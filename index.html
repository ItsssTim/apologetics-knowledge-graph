The structured argument map of contemporary evangelical apologetics.

Synthesised from 301 talks, debates, and systematic-theology lectures by eight contemporary, broadly evangelical apologists: William Lane Craig, John Lennox, Wes Huff, Stephen Meyer, Jonathan McLatchie, Cliffe Knechtle, Preston Perry, and Lee Strobel. It maps their case across both popular apologetics and systematic teaching, covering arguments, objections, responses, and the gaps they leave open.
